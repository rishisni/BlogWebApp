About The Soul Talk

The Soul Talk is a literary platform founded by Ashish Dwivedi, an Author and Literature enthusiast, in 2018. Over the past 5 years, we've organized 500+ events across India, both online and offline, connecting over 20,000 creators from 50+ cities.

Our vision is to promote art and literature through various initiatives such as poetry competitions, events, and celebrating the works of legendary poets. We aim to provide a platform for every individual who wishes to express themselves through writing.

If you are a writer seeking a platform or support, we are here to help you on your creative journey.
