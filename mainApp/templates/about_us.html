About The Soul Talk

The Soultalk is a 5 years old literary platform founded by Ashish Dwivedi, an author and literature lover, in 2018. We do events all over India in different cities. Till now, we have done more than 500 events, both online and offline, and connected over 20K creators from more than 50 cities in India.

Our vision is to promote art and literature through various means such as organizing poetry competitions, poetry events, and celebrating the works of legendary poets. Our mission is to provide a literature platform to every human being who wants to express themselves and achieve something amazing in the field of writing.

If you are a writer and looking for a platform or any kind of support, we are here to help you.
